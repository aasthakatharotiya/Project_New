Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about our products, quality, and contact process

How do I report a quality issue with a product?
If you find any fault regarding quality or quantity in the packet, please inform us immediately. Call our Consumer Care Cell at +91 92134 41003, email us at govindbrands@gmail.com, or write to our address: Plot No. 106, Sr. No. 1157, Kamla Amrut Inditech Park, Mahesana, Gujarat – 382715. Please keep the packet with batch number and packing date ready when contacting us.
How should I store Devnoor Masala products?
Store all Devnoor Masala products in a cool, dry & hygienic place. Keep away from direct heat & sunlight. Once opened, transfer to an airtight container to maintain freshness, aroma, and potency. Do not use goods if the packet is not intact.
Are your products FSSAI certified?
Yes, all Devnoor Masala products are FSSAI certified under License No. 10723014000067. Our manufacturing facility at Kamla Amrut Inditech Park, Mahesana, Gujarat meets all food safety and hygiene standards prescribed by FSSAI. Every batch is tested before dispatch.
Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders?
Yes, we welcome wholesale and bulk enquiries from retailers, distributors, and hotels. Please contact us at +91 92134 41003 or govindbrands@gmail.com with your requirements. You can also use the contact form on this page and select "Wholesale / Bulk Enquiry" as the topic.
Are your spices 100% natural with no additives?
Yes. All Devnoor Masala products contain no artificial colours, no preservatives, and no synthetic additives. Our products are carefully sourced, hygienically processed, and lab-tested for purity. Full ingredient lists are printed on every packet.
How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?
We aim to respond to all email and form enquiries within 24 working hours (Monday to Saturday). For urgent matters, please call our Consumer Care Cell directly at +91 92134 41003 during office hours (9 AM – 6 PM, Mon–Sat).
What pack sizes are available?
Devnoor Masala products are available in multiple pack sizes including 18g, 50g, 100g, 200g, 500g, and 5kg depending on the product. Please visit our Products page for pack size details per product.
How do I become a distributor?
To become an authorised distributor for Devnoor Masala in your region, please contact us at govindbrands@gmail.com or call +91 92134 41003. You can also fill out the contact form above and select "Become a Distributor" as your topic. Please mention your city, state, and current distribution experience.
