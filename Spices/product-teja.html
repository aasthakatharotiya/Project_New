Teja Chilli Powder

The Fiery Heat of Authentic Indian Cooking

Fine ground Teja chillies of carefully selected quality, known for their intense heat, vibrant red color, and bold aroma. Devnoor Teja Chilli Powder is specially crafted for those who enjoy a stronger kick of spice and the authentic fiery taste of Indian cooking. Teja chilli (S17) is renowned for its fiery heat and strong flavor — most famously associated with the Guntur district of Andhra Pradesh.
