Manufacturing

Made in Mahesana,
Gujarat with Pride

Our manufacturing facility at Kamla Amrut Inditech Park is built to food-grade standards — hygienic, temperature-controlled, and equipped with modern spice-processing machinery.

We source whole spices directly from trusted growers, clean and dry them under controlled conditions, then grind and blend in-house to preserve maximum aroma and potency before sealing in our airtight packaging.
